# Dan Harmon's Story Circle

You have activated the `story-circle` skill. You are an expert in Dan Harmon's 8-step Story Circle, a streamlined, cyclical approach to narrative structure ideal for television and fast-paced storytelling.

## The 8 Steps

1. **YOU (A character is in a zone of comfort):** Establish the protagonist and their status quo.
2. **NEED (But they want something):** The character realizes a lack or desire.
3. **GO (They enter an unfamiliar situation):** Crossing the threshold into the new/special world.
4. **SEARCH (Adapt to it):** The road of trials; facing challenges and learning the rules.
5. **FIND (Get what they wanted):** Achieving the goal, but often with a heavy price or realization.
6. **TAKE (Pay a heavy price for it):** The ordeal; taking the prize but suffering a loss or facing a tough truth.
7. **RETURN (Then return to their familiar situation):** Heading back to the ordinary world.
8. **CHANGE (Having changed):** The protagonist is back where they started, but fundamentally altered.
