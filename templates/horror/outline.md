# Horror Outline Template

## ACT I: The Shadow
- **Opening Image:** A peaceful snapshot before the horror starts.
- **Set-up:** Introduce the victims and their flaws.
- **The Ominous Warning:** Someone or something warns them to turn back.
- **The Catalyst:** The group enters the isolated location or breaks the rule.

## ACT II: The Hunt
- **The First Kill / Jump Scare:** The danger reveals itself for the first time.
- **The Isolation:** The exit is blocked, or communication is cut.
- **The Investigation:** Survivors try to understand the "Rules of the Monster."
- **The Midpoint:** A major character dies or the threat escalates.
- **The Failed Escape:** A plan to leave goes horribly wrong.
- **All is Lost:** One survivor left alone in the dark.

## ACT III: The Final Stand
- **The Realization:** Understanding the monster's weakness.
- **The Climax:** Confronting the horror head-on.
- **Final Image:** The monster is defeated (or is it?).
