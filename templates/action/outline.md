# Action Outline Template

## ACT I
- **Cold Open:** A high-stakes action sequence to hook the audience.
- **The Call to Action:** The hero is hired, attacked, or forced into a mission.
- **The Gear Up:** Equipping the hero for the journey.

## ACT II
- **The First Set Piece:** A mid-level battle or chase.
- **The Mystery:** There is more to the mission than meets the eye.
- **The Midpoint:** A massive explosion or plot twist that raises the stakes.
- **The Chase:** The villains are hot on the hero's trail.

## ACT III
- **The Breach:** Breaking into the villain's stronghold.
- **The Final Showdown:** The ultimate physical or tactical confrontation.
- **The Aftermath:** Surveying the wreckage and the hero's survival.
