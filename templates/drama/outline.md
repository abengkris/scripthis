# Drama Outline Template

## ACT I
- **The Inner Wound:** Show what the protagonist is missing emotionally.
- **The Inciting Incident:** An emotional event forces the hero out of their shell.
- **The Choice:** Deciding to confront the past or a difficult relationship.

## ACT II
- **The Struggle:** Trying to change, but failing due to old habits.
- **The B-Story (The Mirror):** A secondary character shows the hero their own flaws.
- **The Midpoint:** A moment of honesty or a secret revealed.
- **The Downward Spiral:** The hero's relationships begin to crumble.

## ACT III
- **The Epiphany:** The hero realizes what they actually need (not what they want).
- **The Confrontation:** The final emotional talk or sacrifice.
- **The New Self:** The hero is changed, even if they didn't get what they wanted.
