# Sci-Fi Outline Template

## ACT I
- **Opening:** Showcase the futuristic/alien status quo.
- **The Tech Reveal:** Introduce the central scientific concept or gadget.
- **The Glitch:** The technology fails or a discovery challenges reality.

## ACT II
- **The Exploration:** The hero uses or fights against the new reality.
- **The Ethical Conflict:** Is this technology good for humanity?
- **Midpoint:** A discovery about the origin of the world/tech changes everything.
- **Bad Guys Close In:** The corporate/alien/AI threat intensifies.

## ACT III
- **The Solution:** A creative use of the scientific laws to solve the problem.
- **The Climax:** Humanity vs. The Machine / The Stars.
- **The New World:** How the discovery changed the future forever.
