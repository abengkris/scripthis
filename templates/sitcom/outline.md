# Sitcom Outline Template (A-B-C Structure)

## The Setup
- **Cold Open:** A short, funny scene unrelated to the main plot.
- **A-Story Intro:** The protagonist gets a ridiculous goal or enters a misunderstanding.
- **B-Story Intro:** Supporting characters start a minor, often physical, side plot.

## The Complications
- **The C-Story:** A tiny recurring joke throughout the episode.
- **Escalation:** The protagonist's lie or misunderstanding gets bigger.
- **B-Story Peak:** The side plot reaches its funniest moment.

## The Climax
- **The Collision:** The A and B stories accidentally affect each other.
- **The Truth:** The protagonist is forced to admit the truth.
- **The Moral:** A brief moment of growth before everything resets.

## The Button
- **Tag:** A final joke during or after the credits.
